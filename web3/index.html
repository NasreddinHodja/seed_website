S.E.E.D. ZORA COLLECTIVE DROP V1.0

Through S.E.E.D.: ZORA COLLECTIVE DROP V. 0.1, the S.E.E.D. collective proposes a first round of creation and minting of pieces from each of its 9 participants, also inviting 11 other artists/creators to join this creation movement in WEB3, with a focus primarily on the production of Brazilian artists/creators and representatives from the Global South. Unprecedented, the pieces presented in COLLECTIVE DROP V. 0.1 — a total of 20 pieces, from 20 participants — were specially conceived for the occasion, highlighting the specificities of the research, languages, and poetics of each of the creators.



By minting the pieces of S.E.E.D.: ZORA COLLECTIVE DROP V. 0.1, you support the collective and the involved artists/creators in the following ways: Finance the production of artistic works by S.E.E.D. members and invited artists/creators — both in WEB3 and in IRL contexts; Support and enable the growth of our community of Brazilian and Global South artists and creators; Expand the visibility of the S.E.E.D. collective in WEB3 to develop future cultural projects — both in WEB3 and in IRL exhibitions and events; Raise funds for the S.E.E.D. collective to structure itself as a DAO and, once again, continue proposing our projects and empowering others; S.E.E.D.: ZORA COLLECTIVE DROP V. 0.1 is a multifaceted technological creation device, with possibilities for initiation and assembly that are unimaginably complex and surprising, changing with each attempt at connection, depending on the work proposal of each of the participants and the worlds they point towards.



We greatly appreciate your support!
