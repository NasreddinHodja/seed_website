SEED x Bosque Gracias Residency

This joint residency will combine the ideals and goals from both BosqueGracias and S.E.E.D. We will transition through narrative techniques, fabulation and post-cinema, evoking perspectives and decentralized bodies and senses. This collaboration will favor the creation of imagetic hyperlinks manifested across earthly, exploring interdimensional planes and expanding itself from micro to macro levels.

S.E.E.D., conceived as a DAO and an empowering entity for multi-format interdisciplinary experiences, aims to disseminate wisdom, artistic practices, and exhibitions. Their purpose is to bring together immersive environments and develop a curatorial axis of works in NFT and IRL, exploring the unknown and unexplored territories of reality and beyond.

The BosqueGracias Artistic Residence offers a unique experience where artists immerse themselves in nature to find inspiration and share creative experiences with other artists. In this enriching environment, participants explore a variety of workshops and activities designed to foster creativity and the exchange of ideas.This allows a cultural space that seeks to empower artists, local communities, and environmental conservation enthusiasts, while introducing tokenomics in rural areas.
